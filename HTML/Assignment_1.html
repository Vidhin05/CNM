Q1.b¶

$I =-k(plog_{2}p+(1-p)(log_{2}(1-p)))$

$p = 0.5 + \epsilon$

$\therefore $ $I =-k((0.5+\epsilon)log_{2}(0.5+\epsilon)+(0.5-\epsilon)(log_{2}(0.5-\epsilon)))$

$\therefore I = -k((0.5 + \epsilon)(\frac{(\ln(1+ 2\epsilon)}{\ln{2}})+(0.5 - \epsilon)(\frac{(\ln(1- 2\epsilon)}{\ln{2}}))$

$\therefore I = -k((0.5 + \epsilon)(\frac{2\epsilon}{\ln2})+(0.5 - \epsilon)(\frac{-2\epsilon}{\ln2})$

$\therefore I = \frac{-4k(\epsilon)^2}{\ln(2)} +k$

$\therefore I = a -b \epsilon^2$ $ where$ $ a = k$ $ and $ $b = \frac{4k}{\ln(2)}$
